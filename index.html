About

I work for the Center for Watershed Sciences at UC Davis. I'm a GIS project specialist that works on a variety of projects related to water in California. Prior to starting at the Center for Watershed Sciences, I worked as a river scientist at Sierra Streams Institute in Nevada City, CA. I attended Bowdoin College in Brunswick, Maine and graduated in 2011 with a BA in biology with honors and environmental studies.

PISCES is software and data describing the best-known ranges for California's 133 native fish and numerous non-native fish. The data are compiled from multiple sources and are expert verified.

For more information, please visit pisces.ucdavis.edu

Developed a family-level index of biotic integrity (IBI) to assess the response of benthic macroinvertebrate assemblages to anthropogenic disturbances using twelve years of continuous citizen-science monitoring.
