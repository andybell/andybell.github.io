Historical Sacramento - San Joaquin Delta

Historical elevation model of the delta circa 1850's. Built in ArcGIS using a TIN, the 2 meter resolution digital elevation model is used in a 3D-hydrodynamic model to simulate the natural outflows of the Delta prior to human modification.

PISCES - Fish Range and Occurrence Database

PISCES is software and data describing the best-known ranges for California's native fish. The data are compiled from multiple sources and experts and is stored and exported as rangemaps and summary maps.

Rim Fire GigaPans

High-resolution panoramic photos taken at the Tuolumne - Clavey River Confluence. The 2013 Rim Fire was the third largest wildfire in California's History.

Open Layers 3 - Mussels

Open layers map showing mussel farms in Washington State.
