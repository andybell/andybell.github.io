Historical Sacramento - San Joaquin Delta

Historical elevation model of the delta circa 1850's. Built in ArcGIS using a TIN, the 2 meter resolution digital elevation model is used in a 3D-hydrodynamic model to simulate the natural outflows of the Delta prior to human modification.
